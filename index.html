About

Come Again is a modern bar and grill located in the vibrant Kicukiro district of Kigali. We blend traditional Rwandan flavors with contemporary cooking techniques to create a memorable dining experience in a relaxed, welcoming atmosphere.

Why you should go

  • Locally sourced ingredients - Fresh produce from local farmers and markets
  • Expertly crafted cocktails - Creative drinks made by our award-winning mixologists
  • Live music weekends - Enjoy local artists every Friday and Saturday evening
